# Seattle.cool

Welcome to Seattle — the Emerald City of the Pacific Northwest.

## About Seattle

Seattle is a vibrant city known for its stunning natural beauty, innovative tech scene, and rich coffee culture. Nestled between Puget Sound and the Cascade Mountains, it offers breathtaking views and endless outdoor adventures.

## Highlights

| Attraction | Description |
|------------|-------------|
| Space Needle | Iconic 605-foot observation tower |
| Pike Place Market | Historic farmers market since 1907 |
| Museum of Pop Culture | Frank Gehry-designed music museum |
| Olympic Sculpture Park | Free waterfront art park |

## Things to Do

- **Explore** the historic Pioneer Square neighborhood
- **Ride** the ferries across Puget Sound
- **Hike** the trails at Discovery Park
- **Grab coffee** at one of thousands of local cafes
- **Catch a game** at T-Mobile Park or Lumen Field

## Weather

> *"There's no such thing as bad weather, only bad clothing."*
> — Seattle locals

Seattle's reputation for rain is a bit exaggerated — it actually gets less annual rainfall than New York City. The secret? It drizzles often, but rarely pours.
